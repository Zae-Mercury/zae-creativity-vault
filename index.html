2026 · Marketing

Seasonal Brand Associate (Summer)

Old Navy Outlet (GAP Inc.) - McArthur Glen

Promoted and marketed Old Navy products to outlet shoppers during peak summer season. Delivered an excellent customer experience by warmly greeting customers, assisting with product selection and styling recommendations, and ensuring smooth checkout through accurate transaction processing. Supported the store’s commercial goals by maintaining high standards of merchandising—keeping displays organized, stocked, and visually appealing. Focused on providing fast, friendly service while helping the team meet and exceed sales targets

2025 · Entrepreneurship

Founder / RAZA - on Carousell App

Carousell

Built and scaled a secondhand clothings e-commerce resale business from the ground up, achieving ~30% reduction in unused secondhand items within Bambu Apus Ward. Generated over IDR 14 million (~CAD 1,133) in net income within the first 3 months, with consistent month-over-month growth. Championed sustainable consumption practices and circular economy principles within the local community

2024 · Community Service

Pandu Nyawa.ID

MAN 4 Jakarta

Pandu Nyawa, a non-profit initiative in teaching first-aid health and safety, has been central to my personal growth. This group of people bonded by the commitment to empower others, save lives, and create cultures of preparedness against unexpected emergencies such as choking, burnt wounds, and anaphylaxis mitigation. Herein, I'm the workshop leader, organizing this workshop for the young scholars to be educated with life-saving skills-since first aid education equips young scholars with the skills to respond swiftly to emergencies, potentially saving lives in critical situations.

2023 · Community Service

Founder / Cambridge Club

MAN 4 Jakarta

As a co-founder and leader of the Cambridge Club, I created a supportive environment where students could share ideas and develop essential skills. We organized activities ranging from Cambridge exam preparations to speech and debate practices, equipping students for national and international competitions with guidance from a certified trainer. Our embassy visits to Australia, the Netherlands, Canada, and Romania encouraged cross-cultural understanding, while a leadership camp trained students in public speaking and effective teamwork. These experiences underscored the importance of diverse voices and the power of collaboration in achieving common goals.

2023 · Leadership

Intern at Veterinary and Biomedical Hospital

IPB University

At IPB University, I led 27 students in a Biopharmacy program, part of a larger associate of 75 participants. This program included collaborative discussions, internships at veterinary and biomedical hospitals. Provided administrative support and animal retrieval assistance, gaining direct exposure to biomedical clinical environments and interdisciplinary healthcare workflows
